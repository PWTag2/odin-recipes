Applecrisp recipe


More recipes

Apple crisp is a warm dessert made with baked, tender apples topped with a sweet, crunchy, and crumbly topping, typically consisting
of flour, oats, sugar, and butter. It is often flavored with cinnamon and nutmeg and is known for its contrasting textures of soft fruit
and a buttery, crispy topping.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Oats
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk

Directions

  1. Slice apples into very thin cubes
  2. In a hot pan, add apples, oats, brown sugar, and cinnamon, and cook down until mushy
  3. After apples are done, sprinkle a layer of all purpose flour mixed with brown sugar on top
  4. Place it in an oven and broil for 10 minutes
  5. Enjoy either by itself, or with yogurt or ice cream
