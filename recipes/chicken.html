Chicken Recipe


more recipes

Chicken is a very yummy protein, and easy to make. Heres an easy recipe on pan-fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Get a pan very hot
  2. Place chicken seasoned with salt and pepper
  3. Cook for 3 minutes on each side
  4. Then lower the temperature and baste with butter for 5 minutes
  5. Plate and then enjoy
