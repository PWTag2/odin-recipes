Steak Recipe


more recipes

A steak is a delicious way of eating meat.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Salt the steak 30 minutes beforehand
  2. Get a cast iron pan ripping hot
  3. Sear the steak on both sides until crust is formed
  4. Afterwards turn heat down and add butter. Then baste the steak with butter
  5. Once finished, take the steak out of the pan add let rest for 10 minutes before enjoying
